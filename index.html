About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating meaningful social impact across India. Since its establishment, the foundation has worked tirelessly to improve lives through humanitarian initiatives, education, environmental awareness, animal welfare, and women empowerment.

Our mission is to build compassionate communities where every person gets opportunities to grow, learn, and live with dignity.

Our Ongoing Projects

🍛 Project SEVA

Providing food, clothes and essential support to underprivileged families.

📚 Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education and learning opportunities for children.

🐶 Project Jeev

Rescuing, feeding and protecting stray animals.

👩 Project Udaan

Empowering women through education, skill development and employment.

🌱 Project Prakriti

Tree plantation drives and environmental awareness campaigns.

💼 Project Vikas

Helping youth develop professional and leadership skills.
